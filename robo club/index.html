About us

What if you could build ASIMOV or SOPHIA? If you have ever aspired to build your own robot, then the Robotics Society of NIIT University, NUtronix is the place for you! We are a diverse group of students who find roots across various fields. Interested the work we do? We build robots to compete at both national and international events or just out of plain interest. We provide students with the resources to develop robots. Robotics is the branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, structural disposition, manufacture and application of robots. We strive to stimulate interest in robotics among the students of the institute. Besides making a serious endeavour to spread knowledge on Robotics and its diverse applications. It was initiated in 2017 under the tutelage of Prof. Navin Kapur (Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering), with the solitary objective of participating in the WRO. The club today is an active platform for students to display and develop their practical machine building skills and knowledge. We are here to induct you into the rapidly expanding world of robotics. One on one guidance eagerly awaits you!

Mission

We recognize the potential of students in the Middle East and our mission is to bring them together on a common platform with the objective of increasing their awareness and competitive spirit, thus enabling them to be adequately trained to appear and excel in these highly competitive examinations.

Vision

Our Vision is to find roots across various fields of Robotics. We build robots to compete at both national and international events or just out of plain interest. We provide students with the resources to develop robots.
